The history of Turkish coffee

Cafes in the Ottoman Empire , like those around the world, were places where public opinion was formed, being frequented by men. The first cafes were opened in the Ottoman Empire in order to provide services to people waiting for prayer time near mosques.

Yemen coffee

At one time coffee was produced only in Yemen and was brought from there to Ottoman cities. When a coffee seller increased the price of coffee by 100%, he announced this through a notice hanging on the wall : " Coffee comes from Yemen , the distance is much too long, 10 pears are not enough, leave 20 instead."
